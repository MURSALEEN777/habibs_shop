Our Legacy

1960
In 1952, a young boy crossed into Pakistan with his father, carrying tradition instead of belongings. After his father's passing, he moved to Karimabad, Karachi. In 1965, he opened a small shop with his mother's secret achar recipe. This became the foundation of Habib Bhai Achar Waley.
2000
The aroma of spices filled the streets of Karimabad. The shop became famous for its bold and traditional taste. His son joined, preserving every ritual and recipe. The brand became a symbol of heritage and authenticity.
2025
The grandsons expanded the brand across Karachi and beyond. From Daraz to international markets, the legacy grew stronger. Today, the next generation continues the tradition with pride.
